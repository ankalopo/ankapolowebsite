Hi, I'm Anka

I am a multidisciplinary visual artist based in South Florida, USA.

With a foundation in fine arts, I discovered my passion for digital illustration and graphic design during my college years. The ever-evolving world of graphic design has kept me inspired, and my ability to adapt to emerging trends has allowed me to successfully tackle a diverse range of projects. I blend various techniques—such as photography, traditional painting, and both 2D and 3D CGI tools—to create visually compelling and functional solutions.

As a creative professional, I’ve had the privilege of collaborating with clients around the world, across industries like merchandise design, education, entertainment, architecture, and game development, among others.

When I’m not immersed in my work, you’ll find me outdoors hiking, biking, sailing with a crew, or stargazing with my telescope at night.
